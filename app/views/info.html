What is this?

DoctorPricer is a labour of love created to address the problem of finding a GP which fits your budget.

It's completely non-profit to the point that it's negative profit. It's not funded by or afilliated with any government department.

Where's all the data coming from?

In most cases the data comes directly from the websites of PHOs.

You can see the sources used and freshness of the data at stats.doctorpricer.co.nz.

Important Information

  • Fees only apply if you're enrolled in the practice's PHO More
  • Fees are for a standard consult with a GP during standard hours
  • Some practices may have enrolment restrictions
  • Only practices which are currently taking patients are displayed
